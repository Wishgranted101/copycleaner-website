Why ChatGPT Text Formatting Breaks

So what’s actually going on here? The culprit is hidden formatting characters. They're ghosts. Completely invisible in the chat window. But the moment you paste elsewhere? Instant chaos.

Hidden spaces word word AI adds invisible spacing for display alignment

The Four Big ChatGPT Problems

Problem 1: Multiple Spaces

You copy this from ChatGPT:

This text has too many spaces.

You want:

This text has too many spaces.

What causes it: ChatGPT sometimes uses multiple spaces for visual alignment in its interface. Your document treats those as actual space characters.

The fix: CopyCleaner automatically reduces multiple spaces to single spaces while keeping paragraph breaks.

Problem 2: Broken Line Breaks

You copy this:

This sentence starts fine but then
breaks in the middle for no
apparent reason and makes it
hard to read or use anywhere.

You want:

This sentence starts fine but then breaks in the middle for no apparent reason and makes it hard to read or use anywhere.

What causes it: AI tools wrap text to fit their chat window. When you copy, those visual wraps become actual line breaks.

The fix: CopyCleaner detects mid-sentence breaks and removes them. It keeps paragraph breaks where they belong.

Problem 3: Smart Quotes

You copy this:

"These are smart quotes" and they're annoying.

You want:

"These are regular quotes" and they're better.

What causes it: AI tools use typographically "correct" curly quotes. These break in code, plain text editors, and some CMS platforms.

The fix: CopyCleaner converts all curly quotes and apostrophes to straight quotes that work everywhere.

Problem 4: Markdown Formatting

You copy this:

This text has **bold words** and _italic words_ that show up as markdown.

You want:

This text has bold words and italic words that show up as markdown.

What causes it: ChatGPT uses markdown to format text in its interface. When you copy, the markdown syntax comes along.

The fix: CopyCleaner strips markdown formatting like **bold**, _italic_, and other syntax.
