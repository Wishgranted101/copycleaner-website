About CopyCleaner

Making text formatting simple, fast, and private

Our Story

We've all been there: you copy text from a PDF, paste it into an email, and it looks like a formatting disaster. Extra spaces everywhere. Random line breaks in the middle of sentences. Smart quotes that don't work in your system.

The frustration was real. Cmd+Shift+V didn't fix it. Manual editing took forever. We knew there had to be a better way.

That's why we built CopyCleaner.

CopyCleaner is a simple tool that does one thing exceptionally well: it cleans messy text instantly. No complicated settings. No learning curve. Just paste, click, and done.

Our Mission

To save people time and frustration by making text formatting effortless, while respecting their privacy every step of the way.

Why We Built This

As remote work became the norm, we noticed something: people were copying and pasting text more than ever. From PDFs to emails. From ChatGPT to Slack. From websites to documents.

And every time, the formatting was a mess.

Existing solutions were either too complicated, didn't work well, or required uploading your sensitive content to a server (which felt wrong).

We believed there was a better way. A tool that:

  • Works instantly without uploads
  • Respects your privacy completely
  • Solves real formatting problems
  • Costs less than a coffee

So we built it. And now thousands of people use CopyCleaner every day to save time and avoid formatting headaches.
