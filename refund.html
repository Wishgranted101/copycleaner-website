1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, email hello@copycleaner.co within 7 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

1.1 Eligibility

  • Request must be made within 7 days of initial purchase
  • Applies to first-time purchases only
  • Full refund, no questions asked
  • One refund per customer per year

1.2 How to Request

Email us at hello@copycleaner.co with:

  • Your email address associated with the subscription
  • Purchase date
  • Brief reason for refund (optional, helps us improve)

1.3 Processing Time

  • Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refund issued to original payment method
  • You will receive confirmation email

2. Non-Refundable Situations

2.1 After 7 Days

  • No refunds after 7-day guarantee period
  • Unused portions of subscription are non-refundable
  • Cancel anytime to prevent future charges

2.2 Abuse or Violations

  • Refunds denied for Terms of Service violations
  • Refunds denied for fraudulent activity
  • Refunds denied for excessive refund requests

2.3 Third-Party Fees

  • Payment processing fees are non-refundable
  • Currency conversion fees are non-refundable

3. How to Cancel Your Subscription

Easy cancellation through Lemon Squeezy:

  1. Check your subscription receipt email from Lemon Squeezy
  2. Click "Manage Subscription"
  3. You can cancel, pause, or modify your plan anytime
  4. No need to contact us
  5. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period

3.1 After Cancellation

  • Access continues until end of paid period
  • No refund for remaining days
  • No auto-renewal charges after cancellation
  • Can reactivate subscription anytime

4. Failed Payments

4.1 Payment Failures

  • Service suspended if payment fails
  • 7-day grace period to update payment method
  • Account deleted after 30 days of non-payment
  • No refunds for service interruption due to failed payment

4.2 Disputed Charges

  • Contact us at hello@copycleaner.co before disputing with bank

5.1 Discount Codes

  • Promotional rates may expire upon refund

6. Technical Issues

6.1 Service Outages

6.2 Browser Compatibility
